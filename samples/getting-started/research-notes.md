# Sample Notebook – Alpine Plant Adaptations

This short document demonstrates how Offline Notebook LM ingests and retrieves content.

## Key Points
- Alpine plants endure intense UV radiation, freeze-thaw cycles, and thin soils.
- Cushion morphology creates a warm microclimate that protects meristems.
- Antifreeze proteins prevent intracellular ice formation and reduce cellular damage.
- Mycorrhizal partnerships accelerate nutrient uptake in rocky terrain.
